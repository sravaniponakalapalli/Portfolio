Human Resources Dashboard

Project Overview

This Human Resources Dashboard is designed to provide insights into key HR metrics and trends within an organization. It helps HR professionals and stakeholders understand employee demographics, attrition patterns, and satisfaction levels through interactive and intuitive visualizations.

Dashboard Features

The dashboard consists of three main pages, each focusing on a specific aspect of HR analysis.

Employee Analysis

  • Visualizes total employees by marital status, department, education level, and age group.
  • Provides slicers for gender, job role, department, and age group to enable dynamic exploration.

Attrition Analysis

  • Displays attrition rate by department, gender, years at the company, and job role.
  • Helps identify patterns and high-risk employee segments contributing to attrition.
  • Includes slicers for gender, job role, department, and age group.

Satisfaction Analysis

  • Analyzes employee satisfaction levels across multiple dimensions.
  • Highlights areas of concern and opportunities for improving workplace engagement.

Key Metrics

The dashboard presents the following KPIs using card visuals:

  • Total Employees: Total number of employees in the organization.
  • Attrition Count: Number of employees who have left the organization.
  • Attrition Rate: Percentage of employees who have exited.
  • Average Age: Average age of employees.
  • Average Tenure: Average number of years employees have been with the organization.
  • Average Income: Average employee income.

Slicers

The following slicers are available across all dashboard pages:

  • Gender
  • Job Role
  • Department
  • Age Group

How to Interpret the Visualizations

  • Employee Analysis: Understand workforce distribution across demographics.
  • Attrition Analysis: Identify trends, risk areas, and potential causes of employee turnover.
  • Satisfaction Analysis: Assess employee satisfaction and engagement levels.

Conclusion

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the organization’s human resources landscape. By combining demographic insights, attrition analysis, and satisfaction metrics, it enables HR professionals to make data-driven decisions aimed at improving employee retention, satisfaction, and overall HR strategy.
